Times Table Trainer

Instructions:

  • When you start the game you will be given a certain amount of time to factor the number given.
  • You enter the factors via the input box (Use your enter key to confirm selection).
  • You can only input factors 2 - 12 for the given number.
  • When you input all of the factors of the given number you move on to the next round.
  • Your best win-streak is tallied.
  • The board is only used as a reference.
  • Dont forget to choose your difficulty.
